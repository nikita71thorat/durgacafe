Best Franchise Chain for Investment By Economic Times

Café Durga was awarded as the best franchise chain for investment at the most prestigious annual Economic Times Award ceremony on 22nd December 2021. The event was held at Hayatt Regency, Pune and it was a grand festival of successful business stories.

The Cafe Durga business model was thoroughly studied and was determined to be superior to all of its rivals. A jury member applauded Cafe Durga for their risk analysis and optimum use of resources. An eminent jury consisting of prominent members from the finance world interviewed co-founder Mr. Shashank Mengade to understand the business model well.

The financial management of Cafe Durga during tough COVID times impressed each and every jury member. Survey also included actual case study of various franchise and it was noted that almost all of the franchise are doing exceptionally well financially. Jury members also noted that Cafe Durga had the best numbers when it came to customer satisfaction also, due minimum risk and maximum returns Cafe Durga came out as a winner among tough competitors and the eminent jury came to a conclusion that Cafe Durga is the best franchise chain for investment.
