Misal Pav is a VERY FAMOUS Dish In Maharashtra, Specially The Puneri
And Kolhapuri Misal. The Ingredients Are Arranged In Multiple Tier
Fashion And Served, Misal Is Served with Sliced Bread Or A Small Loaf, In
The Dish MisalPav. The Main Part Of The Misal Is The Spicy Curry, Called
Tarry, Kat Or Misal Sample.
